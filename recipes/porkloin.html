Air Fryer Pork Loin

Description

This is a great recipe for air fryer pork loin. I removed an ingredient or two to my liking

Ingredients

  • 1.25-1.75lbs Pork Tenderloin
  • 2 Tbsp Brown Sugar
  • 1 Tbsp Smoked Paprika
  • 1.5 Tsp Salt
  • 1/2 Tsp Onion Powder
  • 1/4 Tsp
  • 1/2 Tbsp Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Mix all dry ingredients into a bowl
  2. Remove any excess fat and silver skin from loin
  3. Coat loin with Olive Oil
  4. Rub dry ingredient mix all over loin
  5. Preheat air fryer to 400F for 5 minutes
  6. Cook loin at 400F for 22 minutes
  7. When complete, internal temp of loin should be 165F
  8. Place on cutting and let sit for 5 minutes
  9. Cut and serve

Enjoy!
